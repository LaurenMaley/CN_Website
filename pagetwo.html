2.2 Describe whether the IT tools selected were appropriate

In regards to the use of Trello for my projects, I believe it to be significantly more useful for team projects as opposed to individual projects. This applies for me personally, as I am a rather motivated person who is able to self-start and work autonomously. When using Trello for team work it enables you to track your own progress -as much as anyone else's- and when working to a deadline as part of a team this is more important. Whereas the ability to self-manage means that for me personally Trello is not as effectively implemented in an individual project. However, if organisation is something you have difficulty with then Trello would also be useful in individual projects. When working with VSC, I believe it was the most appropriate programme to use in comparison to online alternatives as it’s offline functionality allows for continuous offline productivity, and therefore does not rely on the need for constant online connection. Additionally, as previously mentioned, the ability to change code aesthetics within the programme was useful for finding the visual style that helped me work most efficiently.
